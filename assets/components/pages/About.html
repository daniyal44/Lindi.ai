Our Story

Founded in 2008, MDK Gallery began as a small creative studio with a passion for visual storytelling. What started with just three designers has now grown into a multidisciplinary team of 30+ creative professionals.

Over the years, we've evolved from a traditional design studio into a full-service creative agency, embracing new technologies while staying true to our core values of creativity, quality, and client collaboration. Our journey has taken us from local projects to international campaigns, working with brands across 15 countries.
